The gastronomy of Honduras

Honduran gastronomy is very varied, as it contains indigenous pre-Columbian, Spanish, Creole and, to some extent, African elements, as is typical throughout the Central American Atlantic coast. Honduras tiene una rica variedad de comida, misma que disfrutan mucho los hondureños y a la vez, conquista los corazones de una alta cantidad de extranjeros.
